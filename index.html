1) 2 Minute Noodles.

  • Time: 3 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients | Quantity
Noodles 500g
Flavouring 10g
Water 250 ml

Step 1: Put the noodles and flavouring in a heat proof bowl.
Step 2: Boil water.
Step 3: Poir boiling water into the bowl.
Step 4: Put in microwave for 2 minutes.
Step 5: Enjoy.
